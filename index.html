About GRIND & SHINE

Welcome to GRIND & SHINE, your premier mobile car wash and detailing service serving Baytown and surrounding areas. Founded by Josh Johnson, a certified Car Detailing Specialist with years of experience, we bring professional-grade cleaning services right to your doorstep.

Our mission is simple: provide top-quality car care with convenience you deserve. No need to waste time traveling - we come to you with all the equipment and expertise to make your vehicle shine like new.

"WE'LL COME TO YOU!" isn't just our slogan - it's our promise of convenience and exceptional service.
